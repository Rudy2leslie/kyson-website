Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is your MOQ?

Our MOQ varies by product: Paper cups typically 30,000 pcs, bowls 10,000-50,000 pcs. Contact us for specific product MOQ.

Do you offer samples?

Yes, we provide free samples for qualified orders. Sample shipping cost is borne by the customer.

What is the lead time?

Standard products: 15-25 days. Custom printing: 25-35 days depending on order quantity and complexity.

What payment terms?

We accept T/T, L/C at sight. For small orders, 30% deposit, 70% before shipment.

Can you do custom printing?

Yes, we offer flexo and offset printing up to 6 colors. Free design support available.

Do you have certifications?

Yes, we are BSCI, FSC, FSSC22000, and DIN CERTCO certified. Certificates available upon request.
