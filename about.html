About Us

Your trusted partner for sustainable paper packaging solutions since 2009

Leading Paper Packaging Manufacturer

Hunan Kyson Tech Co., Ltd. is a professional manufacturer specializing in eco-friendly paper packaging products. Since our establishment in 2009, we have been committed to providing high-quality, sustainable packaging solutions to customers worldwide.

With a factory area of over 60,000 square meters and 15 advanced production lines, we have the capacity to produce more than 800,000 cases annually. Our products are exported to over 50 countries across Europe, North America, Asia, and beyond.

We hold multiple international certifications including BSCI, FSC, FSSC22000, and DIN CERTCO, ensuring our products meet the highest standards of quality and sustainability.

Our Certifications

Meeting international standards for quality and sustainability

BSCI

Business Social Compliance Initiative certification ensures ethical business practices and worker welfare standards throughout our supply chain.

FSC

Forest Stewardship Council certification guarantees our paper products come from responsibly managed forests that provide environmental benefits.

FSSC22000

Food Safety System Certification ensures our products meet the highest food safety standards for direct food contact applications.

DIN CERTCO

German certification for compostable products, verifying our biodegradable packaging meets strict European standards.

AS 5810

Australian Standard for biodegradable plastics, ensuring our products break down naturally in composting environments.

ISO 9001

Quality management system certification demonstrating our commitment to consistent quality and continuous improvement.
