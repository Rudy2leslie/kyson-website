Sample Policy

We understand the importance of evaluating our products before placing a large order. Our sample policy is designed to help you make informed decisions.

Free Samples

Free samples are available for qualified orders. We provide existing samples from our current product range at no cost (shipping charges apply).

Custom Samples

For custom printed samples, we charge a sample fee which will be deducted from your bulk order. Custom sample lead time is 7-10 days.

Shipping

Sample shipping costs are borne by the customer. We ship via DHL, FedEx, or UPS for fast delivery (3-7 days worldwide).
